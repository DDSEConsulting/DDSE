Granted Patent

We solely own the granted patent: "Data Driven Computing System Development" specialized in computing real-time system software development and safety mechanisms for the following fields, and we welcome the collaborations from all relevant fields' engineers / experts, IP attornes and products' makers.

1. Automotive

  • Applications: Engine control units (ECUs), Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS), infotainment systems, electronic stability control, and electric vehicle (EV) battery management.
  • Standards/Regulations: ISO 26262 (Functional Safety), ASPICE, UN ECE R155/R156 (Cybersecurity).

2. Aerospace and Aviation

  • Applications: Flight control systems, avionics, autopilots, navigation systems, and radar systems.
  • Standards/Regulations: DO-178C (Software for Airborne Systems), DO-254 (Hardware), ARINC standards.

3. Maritime and Shipping

  • Applications: Ship navigation systems, engine control, ballast water management, and collision avoidance systems.
  • Standards/Regulations: IMO (International Maritime Organization) regulations, IEC 60945 (Maritime Navigation and Communication Equipment).

4. Medical Devices

  • Applications: MRI machines, infusion pumps, pacemakers, ventilators, and surgical robots.
  • Standards/Regulations: IEC 62304 (Medical Device Software), FDA 21 CFR Part 11.

5. Industrial Automation

  • Applications: Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs), robotic arms, conveyor systems, and machine monitoring systems.
  • Standards/Regulations: IEC 61508 (Functional Safety for Industrial Equipment).

6. Consumer Electronics

  • Applications: Smartphones, smart home devices (IoT), gaming consoles, and wearable technology.
  • Standards: Embedded Linux, RTOSs like FreeRTOS, or proprietary systems.

7. Energy and Utilities

  • Applications: Smart grid systems, wind turbine controllers, nuclear reactor control systems, and solar panel inverters.
  • Standards/Regulations: IEC 61850 (Communication Networks for Power Systems), IEEE 1547 (Interconnection Standards).

8. Telecommunications

  • Applications: Base stations, satellite communication systems, and network routers.
  • Standards: 5G, LTE, IEEE standards for communication protocols.

9. Defense and Military

  • Applications: Missile guidance systems, radar systems, UAVs (drones), and tactical communication systems.
  • Standards/Regulations: MIL-STD-498 (Software Development), DO-178C.

10. Rail and Transportation Systems

  • Applications: Train control systems, signaling systems, automatic braking systems.
  • Standards/Regulations: EN 50128 (Railway Software Systems).

11. Agriculture and Heavy Equipment

  • Applications: Autonomous tractors, combine harvesters, and precision farming systems.
  • Standards/Regulations: ISO 25119 (Agricultural Machinery).

12. Financial Technology (FinTech)

  • Applications: High-frequency trading systems, ATMs, point-of-sale (POS) systems.
  • Standards: PCI DSS (Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard).

13. Robotics

  • Applications: Industrial robots, service robots (cleaning, delivery), and autonomous drones.
  • Standards/Regulations: ISO 10218 (Safety of Industrial Robots).

14. Entertainment and Media

  • Applications: Video streaming devices, sound systems, and AR/VR devices.
  • Standards: Embedded OS frameworks for real-time rendering and audio processing.

15. Retail and Logistics

  • Applications: Warehouse management systems, automated inventory systems, and self-checkout kiosks.
  • Standards: IoT and RFID integration.

16. Space Exploration

  • Applications: Satellite systems, space probes, and launch vehicle systems.
  • Standards/Regulations: NASA-STD-8719.13 (Software Safety).
