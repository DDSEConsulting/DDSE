Granted Patent

We solely own the granted patent: "Data Driven Computing System Development" specialized in computing real-time system software development and safety mechanisms for the following fields. The present invention is based on the data value and data timing attributes realizations and provides an explicitly defined definite or fixed method for constructing a computing system software consisting of:

(1). A definite system development method;

(2). A definite safety relevant system development method in the furthermore manner;

(3) the specific and definite criteria to measure the software and the safety.

The features above not only standardize the developments to make them more efficient, but also make the AI powered software development automation feasible and practical.

We welcome the collaborations from all relevant fields' engineers / experts, IP attorneys and products' developers to promote the innovation and to make the development much more efficient.

* Automotive

  • Applications: Engine control units (ECUs), Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS), infotainment systems, electronic stability control, and electric vehicle (EV) battery management.
  • Standards/Regulations: ISO 26262 (Functional Safety), ASPICE, UN ECE R155/R156 (Cybersecurity).

* Aerospace and Aviation

  • Applications: Flight control systems, avionics, autopilots, navigation systems, and radar systems.
  • Standards/Regulations: DO-178C (Software for Airborne Systems), DO-254 (Hardware), ARINC standards.

* Maritime and Shipping

  • Applications: Ship navigation systems, engine control, ballast water management, and collision avoidance systems.
  • Standards/Regulations: IMO (International Maritime Organization) regulations, IEC 60945 (Maritime Navigation and Communication Equipment).

* Medical Devices

  • Applications: MRI machines, infusion pumps, pacemakers, ventilators, and surgical robots.
  • Standards/Regulations: IEC 62304 (Medical Device Software), FDA 21 CFR Part 11.

* Industrial Automation

  • Applications: Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs), robotic arms, conveyor systems, and machine monitoring systems.
  • Standards/Regulations: IEC 61508 (Functional Safety for Industrial Equipment).

* Energy and Utilities

  • Applications: Smart grid systems, wind turbine controllers, nuclear reactor control systems, and solar panel inverters.
  • Standards/Regulations: IEC 61850 (Communication Networks for Power Systems), IEEE 1547 (Interconnection Standards).

* Robotics

  • Applications: Industrial robots, service robots (cleaning, delivery), and autonomous drones.
  • Standards/Regulations: ISO 10218 (Safety of Industrial Robots).
